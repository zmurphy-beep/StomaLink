Our story

StomaLink by Gutsy LLC was created to connect and support people living with ostomies and digestive conditions through real experiences, reliable guidance, and a community that ensures no one has to face it alone. The idea came from firsthand experience—after a severe diverticulitis flare led to emergency surgery, complications, and months living with an ileostomy, the reality of daily challenges, isolation, and uncertainty became clear. What made the difference was having support, people willing to help, and access to real-world advice. StomaLink exists to bring that same level of support to others—offering a place to share experiences, find practical solutions, and build connections with people who truly understand. The goal is simple: reach those going through similar struggles and help them navigate their journey with strength, confidence, and the support they deserve.

Privacy policy

StomaLink is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect only the information necessary to provide our services. Your personal health information is never sold or shared with third parties. All support messages are handled with complete confidentiality.

Terms of use

By using StomaLink you agree to treat all community members with respect and compassion. Content that is harmful, hateful, or medically misleading will be removed. StomaLink is a community platform and does not provide medical advice — always consult your healthcare provider.
